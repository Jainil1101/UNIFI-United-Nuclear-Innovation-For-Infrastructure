What am I looking at? These are modelled figures, not a live feed. The SIMULATED values are calculated from the Akademik Lomonosov's published specifications (steady ~90% output) — a typical single day of operation, not real-time telemetry. Only Site Conditions is genuinely live, fetched from the open Open-Meteo weather API. When a real plant is built and operating, its own certified sensor feed would replace this model — the page layout stays the same.
